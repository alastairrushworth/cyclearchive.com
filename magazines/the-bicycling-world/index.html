The Bicycling World

The Bicycling World was published bi-weekly by Charles E. Pratt and The Bicycling World Co. at 40 Water St., Boston, Massachusetts. The first issue appeared in November 1879, priced at $2.50 per year. It was one of the first dedicated bicycling periodicals in the United States. The magazine covered race results, club news, touring information, and technical developments in early American cycling.
