The Velocipedist

The Velocipedist was a monthly magazine published in New York by Pickering & Davis beginning in early 1869. It was edited by W. Chester King and ran for three monthly issues during America's brief velocipede craze (fall 1868 to spring 1869). The eight-page quarto format publication covered velocipede news and developments.
